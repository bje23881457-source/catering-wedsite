Our Story

Catering of true dignity,
born in Gyeongju, the millennial capital

The Silla Table is a premium catering brand that translates the cultural depth of Gyeongju and Silla into fine cuisine. Beyond simply serving food, we design experiences that match the purpose and stature of each event.

Combining the culinary expertise of a chef with nearly 30 years on the front line and the operational capability to manage the logistics, staffing and flow of large-scale events, we have reliably delivered events of every size for academic, corporate and government clients.

In particular, using highly pre-production-stable cooking techniques, we uphold quality and on-time delivery even at multi-day events of thousands of guests — aiming to be a warm partner that procurement managers can entrust with confidence.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum headcount and scale you handle?
We handle everything from small gatherings to large-scale events of thousands of guests. We provide tailored proposals based on the nature and budget of your event, so please let us know your expected headcount when requesting a quote.
How far in advance should I inquire?
Large-scale, multi-day events require pre-production as well as staffing and logistics preparation, so the more lead time you can give, the better. For urgent schedules, we will promptly advise on availability.
Do you accommodate special diets such as halal or vegetarian?
Yes. We can reflect special dietary requirements including halal, vegetarian/vegan and allergy needs, and we also support multilingual service for international groups.
Can you issue government quotations and documentation?
As an officially accredited business holding Certified Women-Owned Business status, we provide the clear quotations and supporting documents required for government and public events.
Which areas do you cover for on-site service?
Based in Gyeongju, we provide on-site catering across all of Daegu and Gyeongbuk. Other regions may also be possible depending on scale, so please get in touch.
